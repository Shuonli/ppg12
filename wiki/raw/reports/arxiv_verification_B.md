### 1. `0903.3399.pdf`

- **Claimed:** PHENIX, Photon-hadron correlations pp+AuAu 200 GeV, PRC 80 (2009)
- **Verified title:** "Photon-Hadron Jet Correlations in p+p and Au+Au Collisions at sqrt(s_NN) = 200 GeV"
- **Authors/collaboration:** A. Adare et al. (PHENIX Collaboration)
- **arXiv:** 0903.3399v1 [nucl-ex], 19 Mar 2009
- **Journal ref (from arXiv ID):** Phys. Rev. C 80, 024908 (2009)
- **Page count:** 14 pages
- **Abstract summary:** Reports suppression of back-to-back correlations in the direct photon+jet channel in Au+Au relative to p+p. Away-side I_AA = 0.32 +/- 0.12(stat) +/- 0.09(syst) for central Au+Au. Direct photon associated yields in p+p scale with z_T.
- **Match: YES.** Title, collaboration, collision systems (pp and AuAu at 200 GeV), and journal all match the claim.
