## What Is Known from Other Sources

- Referenced as Ref. [8] in CDF 0910.3623 (PRD 80, 2009): "F. Abe et al., (CDF Collaboration), Phys. Rev. D 48, 2998 (1993)"
- In 0910.3623, the CES shower-profile method from this 1993 paper is cited as an alternative purity-determination technique, used as a systematic cross-check for the newer isolation template fit method
- This paper predates the isolation-template and ABCD methods; likely used CES shower-profile-based photon identification as the primary background subtraction method
- Measured at 1.8 TeV rather than the later Run II energy of 1.96 TeV
- Listed as Tier 1 (optional reading) in PPG12 reading list under category A3 (Tevatron)

## Relevance to PPG12

- **Historical context**: One of the earliest Tevatron prompt photon measurements; established baseline techniques later refined by the 1.96 TeV measurements from both CDF and D0
- **CES shower-profile method**: The shower-shape-based purity determination introduced here is a precursor to the BDT-based approach used in PPG12
- **Low priority**: At Tier 1, this paper provides historical context but is not essential for the PPG12 analysis or wiki
