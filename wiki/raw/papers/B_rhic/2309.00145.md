# Semi-inclusive direct photon+jet and pi0+jet correlations measured in p+p and central Au+Au collisions at sqrt(s_NN) = 200 GeV

## Abstract

The STAR experiment reports new measurements of jet quenching based on the semi-inclusive distribution of charged-particle jets recoiling from direct photon (gamma_dir) and neutral pion (pi0) triggers in p+p and central Au+Au collisions at sqrt(s_NN) = 200 GeV, for triggers in the range 9 < E_T^trig < 20 GeV. Jets are reconstructed using the anti-k_T algorithm with resolution parameters R = 0.2 and 0.5. Comparison of distributions measured in p+p and Au+Au reveals strong medium-induced jet yield suppression for R = 0.2, with markedly less suppression for R = 0.5.

## Key Results

- Semi-inclusive recoil-jet distributions reported for p_T,jet^ch < 25 GeV/c in both p+p and central Au+Au
- Three trigger kinematic ranges: pi0 (9 < p_T < 11, 11 < p_T < 15 GeV/c) and gamma_dir (9 < E_T < 11, 11 < E_T < 15, 15 < E_T < 20 GeV)
- Relative quark fraction in recoil partons is significantly larger for gamma_dir triggers than pi0 triggers at same E_T (PYTHIA-6 calculation)
- gamma_dir triggers provide unbiased in-medium path-length distribution; pi0 triggers biased toward larger path lengths (surface emission effect)
- I_AA for R = 0.2 decreases with increasing p_T,jet^ch and is significantly below unity for p_T,jet^ch > 10 GeV/c; I_AA is larger for R = 0.5 than R = 0.2, indicating energy recovery at large angles
- I_AA distributions for gamma_dir and pi0 triggers are consistent within uncertainties, but steeper spectrum shape for gamma_dir implies larger mean energy loss for pi0-triggered recoil (more gluon jets)
- R_{0.2/0.5} in p+p is less than unity for p_T,jet^ch > 7 GeV/c, well described by PYTHIA-6 STAR tune; marked suppression in Au+Au relative to p+p indicates medium-induced intra-jet broadening
- Five theoretical models compared (Jet-fluid, LBT, CoLBT-hydro, SCET, Hybrid): back-reaction implementation is key distinction; none fully consistent with all observables simultaneously
- Hybrid model (with or without wake) underpredicts medium-induced intra-jet broadening at angular scales R ~ 0.5
- LBT model likewise predicts R_{0.2/0.5} greater than observed for gamma_dir trigger
- Comparison with STAR h+jet I_CP shows consistent jet quenching signal across trigger types

## Methodology

### Photon Identification and Trigger Selection
- STAR Barrel Electromagnetic Calorimeter (BEMC, |eta| < 1.0, full azimuth) for triggering
- L2Gamma trigger: high-tower E_T > 4.2 GeV (p+p) / 5.9 GeV (Au+Au), plus 3x3 tower cluster sum E_T > 7.44 GeV
- Barrel Shower Maximum Detector (BSMD) for gamma/pi0 discrimination via transverse shower profile
- gamma_dir fraction determined statistically: direct photon purity measured via correlated charged-track rate (p_T > 1.2 GeV/c) in near-side, assuming zero associated rate for true direct photons
- Corrected gamma_dir distributions from linear combination of gamma_rich and pi0-triggered distributions
- No isolation cut applied; fragmentation photon contribution suppressed but not fully subtracted

### Jet Reconstruction
- Charged-particle jets via anti-k_T with R = 0.2 and 0.5
- Primary tracks from TPC (|eta| < 1.0, 0.2 < p_T < 30 GeV/c, DCA < 1 cm, >= 15 TPC hits)
- E-scheme recombination, active ghost area 0.01
- Recoil jet acceptance: Delta_phi in [3pi/4, 5pi/4], |eta_jet| < 1.0 - R
- Area cuts: A_jet > 0.05 (R=0.2), A_jet > 0.65 (R=0.5)
- p_T corrected by subtracting background density rho times jet area

### Semi-Inclusive Analysis
- Trigger-normalized recoil-jet distribution = ratio of triggered+jet cross section to trigger inclusive cross section
- Selection bias only from trigger choice (no cuts on jet population beyond acceptance)
- Mixed-event (ME) procedure for Au+Au: normalizes ME distribution in large-negative p_T,jet^reco region where uncorrelated yield dominates
- Vertical correction (uncorrelated yield subtraction) followed by horizontal correction (p_T smearing unfolding)
- Closure tests validate analysis chain with embedded PYTHIA jets in Au+Au data

### Theoretical Model Comparisons
- Jet-fluid: PYTHIA + VISHNU 2+1D hydro, HT energy loss, no back-reaction
- LBT: PYTHIA + CLVisc 3+1D hydro, elastic + inelastic (HT), thermal recoil rescattering
- CoLBT-hydro: extends LBT with jet-induced medium excitation in hydro evolution
- SCET: PYTHIA + 2+1D viscous hydro, GLV first-order opacity, variable coupling
- Hybrid: PYTHIA + hydro, holographic energy loss, optional wake generation

### Data
- p+p: 11.3M L2Gamma events, 23 pb^-1 after offline cuts (2009 RHIC run)
- Au+Au: 3.9 nb^-1 after offline cuts (2014 RHIC run), central 0-15%
- Centrality from TPC global track multiplicity in |eta| < 0.5
- Pileup rejection via vertex-position comparison (TPC vs VPD, < 6 cm difference)
- PYTHIA-6 STAR tune, Perugia 0, Perugia 2012, and PYTHIA-8 Monash used for various analysis elements

### Systematic Uncertainties
- p+p pi0-triggered: cumulative 7-16% depending on p_T,jet and R (dominated by instrumental response)
- p+p gamma_dir-triggered: cumulative 10-143%, dominated by purity uncertainty at low E_T (80-81% for 9-11 GeV); manageable (13-21%) for 15-20 GeV
- Au+Au: additional ME normalization and unfolding systematics; tracking efficiency (55-82% depending on p_T and system)
- Instrumental systematic from tracking resolution parameterization: sigma_pT = sigma_0 + sigma_1 * p_T + sigma_2 * p_T^2
- Closure tests with embedded PYTHIA jets in Au+Au data validate the full analysis chain

## Relevance to PPG12

- This is the most detailed published account of photon identification and direct photon+jet analysis at sqrt(s) = 200 GeV at RHIC, directly relevant to the PPG12 photon selection strategy
- The statistical subtraction method for gamma_dir purity (using near-side associated tracks) provides context for why PPG12 instead uses shower-shape BDT + isolation + ABCD, which gives event-by-event photon ID
- The BEMC shower-shape analysis for gamma/pi0 discrimination is a precursor to the sPHENIX EMCal approach that PPG12 extends with multivariate BDT
- The mixed-event technique for background subtraction in heavy-ion is relevant context for PPG12, though PPG12 focuses on p+p where this is less critical
- Demonstrates that charged-particle jets at RHIC can be measured down to ~10 GeV/c, relevant for understanding the jet recoil spectrum opposite PPG12 photons
- PYTHIA-6 STAR tune validation for p+p photon+jet at this energy is relevant for PPG12 MC comparisons
- The 23 pb^-1 p+p luminosity and trigger scheme provide a benchmark for the sPHENIX data-taking strategy
