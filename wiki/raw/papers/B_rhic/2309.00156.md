# Measurement of in-medium jet modification using direct photon+jet and pi0+jet correlations in p+p and central Au+Au collisions at sqrt(s_NN) = 200 GeV

## Abstract

The STAR Collaboration presents measurements of the semi-inclusive distribution of charged-particle jets recoiling from energetic direct-photon (gamma_dir) and neutral-pion (pi0) triggers in p+p and central Au+Au collisions at sqrt(s_NN) = 200 GeV over a broad kinematic range, for jet resolution parameters R = 0.2 and 0.5. Medium-induced jet yield suppression is observed to be larger for R = 0.2 than for 0.5, reflecting the angular range of jet energy redistribution due to quenching. Predictions of model calculations incorporating jet quenching are not fully consistent with the observations.

## Key Results

- First gamma_dir+jet measurement with reconstructed jets at RHIC
- I_AA (recoil-jet yield suppression) is markedly larger for R = 0.2 than R = 0.5, providing new measurement of angular scale of jet energy redistribution by quenching
- R_{0.2/0.5} (ratio of recoil-jet yields at R=0.2 vs R=0.5) shows significant medium-induced intra-jet broadening: 0.26 +/- 0.09(sys) for Au+Au vs 0.50 +/- 0.06(sys) for p+p at 10 < p_T,jet^ch < 15 GeV/c
- p+p baseline well described by PYTHIA-6 STAR tune for both gamma_dir and pi0 triggers
- I_AA consistent within uncertainties for gamma_dir and pi0 triggers in common E_T^trig bin, but steeper spectrum shape for pi0 indicates larger average in-medium energy loss
- Trigger kinematics: gamma_dir 15 < E_T^trig < 20 GeV; pi0 11 < p_T^trig < 15 GeV/c
- Recoil jet p_T range extends down to ~10 GeV/c (lower than LHC calorimetric measurements)
- Five jet quenching models compared (Jet-fluid, LBT, CoLBT-hydro, SCET, Hybrid): none fully consistent with all measured distributions

## Methodology

### Photon Identification and gamma_dir/pi0 Discrimination
- STAR Barrel Electromagnetic Calorimeter (BEMC) for triggering and energy measurement
- Barrel Shower Maximum Detector (BSMD) for transverse-shower profile measurement to discriminate single photons from pi0 decays
- pi0 purity >95%
- gamma_dir purity of the gamma-rich population is E_T-dependent: 43-53% in p+p, 67-84% in central Au+Au
- gamma_dir purity measured by rate of correlated charged tracks with p_T > 1.2 GeV/c nearby in phase space, assuming direct-photon triggers have zero correlated rate
- No explicit isolation cut applied; gamma_dir fraction extracted statistically

### Jet Reconstruction
- Charged-particle jets (track jets) using anti-k_T algorithm with R = 0.2 and 0.5
- E-scheme recombination, active ghost area 0.01
- Primary tracks from TPC: |eta| < 1.0, 0.2 < p_T,track < 30 GeV/c
- Tracking efficiency: 72% (Au+Au central, p_T > 2 GeV/c), 82% (p+p)
- Jet acceptance: |eta_jet| < 1.0 - R; area cuts A_jet > 0.05 (R=0.2), A_jet > 0.65 (R=0.5)
- Recoil azimuthal acceptance: 3pi/4 < Delta_phi < 5pi/4

### Background Subtraction
- Mixed-event (ME) procedure for statistical correction of large uncorrelated jet yield in Au+Au
- Background energy density rho subtracted from jet p_T
- Semi-inclusive approach: trigger-normalized jet yield equivalent to ratio of cross sections
- Unfolding correction for p_T smearing from instrumental effects and residual background fluctuations

### Data
- p+p: 23 pb^-1 integrated luminosity (2009 RHIC run)
- Au+Au: 3.9 nb^-1 (2014 RHIC run), central 0-15%
- Online trigger: BEMC high-tower E_T > 4.2 GeV (p+p) or 5.9 GeV (Au+Au), plus 3x3 cluster sum > 7.44 GeV

## Relevance to PPG12

- Same collision energy (sqrt(s) = 200 GeV) and RHIC environment as PPG12
- The BEMC shower-shape discrimination for gamma/pi0 is conceptually similar to the PPG12 BDT approach, though PPG12 uses sPHENIX EMCal with finer granularity
- Demonstrates the importance of precise p+p photon cross section measurements (like PPG12) as the baseline denominator for I_AA calculations
- The 23 pb^-1 p+p luminosity is comparable to the PPG12 dataset (16.6-49.5 pb^-1 depending on crossing angle selection)
- gamma_dir purity challenges at STAR (43-53% in p+p) highlight why PPG12 invests in BDT-based photon ID and isolation cuts to achieve higher purity
- Track-jet approach complements the calorimetric approach that PPG12 uses for photon measurement
- Validates that PYTHIA-6 describes p+p gamma+jet well at RHIC, relevant context for PPG12 MC comparisons
